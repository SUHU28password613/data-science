General Questions

What is MEDICARE+?

MEDICARE+ is a comprehensive telehealth platform that connects patients with healthcare providers remotely. Our services include virtual consultations, electronic prescriptions, health monitoring, and access to your medical records from anywhere.

How do I sign up for MEDICARE+?

You can sign up in three simple steps:

  1. Visit our website and click "Sign Up"
  2. Complete your profile information
  3. Verify your email and identity

Note: Some insurance providers require additional verification steps depending on your coverage.

What devices are supported?

MEDICARE+ is compatible with:

  • Windows PCs (Chrome, Edge, Firefox)
  • Mac computers (Safari, Chrome)
  • Android smartphones and tablets (latest OS versions)
  • iPhones and iPads (iOS 14+)

Insurance Questions

What insurance plans do you accept?

MEDICARE+ accepts most major insurance plans including:

Blue Cross Blue Shield
Aetna
UnitedHealthcare
Medicare

We also accept Medicaid in participating states. Check our coverage tool to verify your specific plan.

How do I change my insurance information?

To update your insurance information:

  1. Log in to your MEDICARE+ account
  2. Navigate to "Profile Settings"
  3. Select "Insurance Information"
  4. Upload a clear image of your insurance card (front and back)
  5. Our system will automatically verify your coverage

Important: Changing insurance may affect appointment availability and copay amounts.

Prescription Questions

Can I get prescriptions through MEDICARE+?

Yes! Our licensed healthcare providers can electronically prescribe medications when clinically appropriate. Here's how it works:

Provider Consultation

Complete a virtual visit with one of our healthcare providers who can assess your needs.

E-Prescription

If needed, your provider will electronically send the prescription to your preferred pharmacy.

Pharmacy Pickup

You'll receive a notification when your prescription is ready for pickup or delivery.

Note: Controlled substances may require additional verification steps per state regulations.

How do I transfer existing prescriptions to MEDICARE+?

Transferring prescriptions is simple:

  1. Request your current pharmacy to transfer prescriptions to your new preferred pharmacy
  2. Inform your MEDICARE+ provider during your appointment
  3. We'll automatically sync with your pharmacy records

Privacy & Security Questions

How does MEDICARE+ protect my health data?

We use enterprise-grade security measures compliant with:

HIPAA Compliance
End-to-End Encryption
Biometric Authentication
SOC 2 Type II Certified
Zero-Trust Architecture
24/7 Monitoring

Your health data is encrypted both in transit and at rest, with strict access controls that limit who can view your information.

Can family members access my account?

For adults (18+), account access is strictly limited to the individual unless you specifically grant access to:

  • Authorized caregivers (requires verification)
  • Healthcare proxies (with legal documentation)
  • Family members through our Family Sharing feature (optional)

For minors, parent/guardian access is automatically enabled and can be managed through the Family Portal.
