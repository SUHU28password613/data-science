Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our doctors and services

Our physician profiles include detailed information about each doctor's specialties, experience, and approach to care. You can use our search filters to find doctors specific to your condition or needs. If you're unsure, our patient navigators can help match you with an appropriate provider.

We accept most major insurance plans including Medicare and Medicaid. Each doctor's profile shows the specific plans they accept. Please contact our billing department or check with your insurance provider to confirm coverage for specific services.

Please bring your insurance card, photo ID, a list of current medications (including dosages), any relevant medical records or test results. New patient forms will be emailed to you after booking and should be completed prior to your visit.

All of our specialists are happy to provide second opinions. For the most productive consultation, bring your complete medical records including test results and imaging reports. If you're transferring care from another provider, we can assist in obtaining your records.
