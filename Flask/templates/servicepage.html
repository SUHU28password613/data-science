Frequently Asked Questions

We accept most major insurance plans including Medicare, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, Cigna, and UnitedHealthcare. Please contact our billing department for specific coverage details.

You can schedule an appointment by calling our office at (555) 123-4567, using our online booking system, or through the patient portal. New patients should arrive 15 minutes early to complete paperwork.

Our emergency department is open 24/7 for urgent medical needs. For life-threatening emergencies, please call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room immediately.

You can access your medical records securely through our patient portal. Alternatively, you may request paper copies by submitting a signed release form at our office. Processing may take 3-5 business days.
