Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

How does AI diagnostics work?

Our AI system analyzes your symptoms, medical history, and available test results against a database of millions of cases. It provides doctors with probable diagnoses and recommended tests, helping them make faster, more accurate decisions.

Is video consultation as effective as in-person visits?

For many conditions, video consultations are equally effective. Doctors can assess visual symptoms, discuss concerns, and provide treatment plans. Some situations may require in-person follow-up, which we can coordinate.

How secure is my medical data?

We use enterprise-grade encryption for all data storage and transmission. Access is strictly controlled and compliant with HIPAA and other privacy regulations. You maintain full ownership of your health records.

Can I get prescriptions filled through this service?

Yes, our licensed physicians can electronically prescribe medications to your preferred pharmacy. Controlled substances may require additional verification.
